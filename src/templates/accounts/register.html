Our registration process

It is not required to own an account to view most of the content on this site. If you do wish to take part in contributing and improving Echelon, please fill in and submit a registration form. Once submitted, a site administrator will need to review your registration request before granting you membership into Echelon.

Did you know?

All account and registration password data is hashed using a secure "Password-Based Key Derivation Function 2" algorithm before stored in our database. This guarantees only you will know your password. But if you forget it, the site administrator cannot recover your password except generate a new one for you.
